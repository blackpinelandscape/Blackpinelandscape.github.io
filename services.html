Services

At Black Pine Landscape, I provide a range of professional tree care services with an emphasis on detail, artistry, and long-term health.

  • Japanese Pruning Traditions
    Sukashi (“see-through pruning”) to reveal structure and light.
    Niwaki shaping for balance, harmony, and timeless beauty.
  • Pruning for Smaller Yard Trees
    Careful, intentional cuts to enhance structure, health, and aesthetics.
  • Fruit Tree Pruning
    Specialized pruning to encourage strong growth, longevity, and productivity.
  • Storm Cleanup & Emergency Tree Work
    Safe removal and chipping of fallen or hazardous trees after severe weather.
